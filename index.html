About Courtney Morrissey:


We'll tell you where to hike in the Denver and Boulder area.

Courtney decided to make this site after fielding regular requests from friends for trail suggestions in the Denver and Boulder foothills. One of the most important features of this site is the capability to redirect hikers away from Chautauqua on weekends, which is a quick way to improve the foothills hiking experience.


Courtney's passions include wolves, wolf shirts, hiking, skiing, paddleboarding, backpacking, and binge watching Friends on Netflix because she is too afraid to get sucked into a new show. She grew up in Upstate New York, lived in Boston, and has now settled in Boulder, Colorado, where she no longer feels out of place in her birken-socks.


Courtney has worked in GIS for the Colorado Division of Water Resources and the US Geological Survey, and is now studying full stack web development at Galvanize gSchool to enhance her career as a GIS developer.
